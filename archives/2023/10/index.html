中文简历

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science Department at Duke University, under the advisement of Prof. Lisa Wu Wills.
My research focus lies at the intersection of computer architecture and machine learning, encompassing two main directions:

  1. Designing efficient computer architecture for machine learning algorithms.
  2. Designing machine learning algorithms for efficient computer architectures.

Before I came to Duke, I received my bachelor degree in Computer Engineering at the University of California, Irvine in 2020.
