About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Computer Science Department, Duke University advised by Prof. Lisa Wu Wills.
My main research interest is about the intersection of computer architecture and machine learning.
There are two main directions I am working on:

  1. Designing efficient computer architecture for machine learning algorithms.
  2. Designing machine learning algorithms for efficient computer architectures.

Before I came to Duke, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Engineering from University of California, Irvine in 2020.
